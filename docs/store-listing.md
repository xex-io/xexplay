## 3. Full Description

Every day, 15 prediction cards. 10 answers. 5 skips. No going back.

XEX Play is the strategic sports prediction game that turns your sports knowledge into real rewards. Each day you receive a curated basket of prediction cards tied to real upcoming matches -- swipe right for Yes, swipe left for No, or swipe up to skip. Every card counts, every decision matters, and the clock is ticking.

HOW IT WORKS

You get 15 prediction cards daily, each linked to a real sports match. Answer 10 and skip 5 -- but choose wisely. Cards are revealed one at a time, and once you decide, there is no going back. A 40-second timer on each card keeps the action moving. Your predictions are scored after matches conclude, and points determine your leaderboard position.

CARD TIERS -- RISK VS. REWARD

Not all cards are equal. Gold cards carry the highest stakes with asymmetric scoring -- one answer is worth far more than the other. Silver cards offer moderate risk with a slight edge on one side. White cards are balanced and safe. With a fixed distribution of 3 Gold, 5 Silver, and 7 White cards each day, planning your skip strategy becomes the core of the game.

LEADERBOARDS AND COMPETITION

Compete on daily, weekly, tournament, and all-time leaderboards. Create or join mini-leagues with friends using invite codes to run private competitions during any tournament. Track your rank in real time and see how you stack up against players worldwide.

STREAK REWARDS

Play every day to build your streak. Hit milestones to unlock bonus skips, bonus answers, and exclusive achievement badges. Miss a day and your streak resets -- the pressure is real.

EARN REAL TOKEN REWARDS

Top leaderboard finishers earn token rewards redeemable on XEX Exchange. Daily prizes keep you coming back, weekly prizes reward consistency, and tournament prize pools create season-long competition with significant rewards. Claim your tokens and trade, hold, or withdraw them on XEX Exchange.

ACHIEVEMENTS AND BADGES

Unlock achievements for milestones like Perfect Day (all 10 predictions correct), streak records, Gold card accuracy, and more. Share your badges on social media with branded visuals optimized for Instagram Stories and Twitter.

MULTI-EVENT SUPPORT

Follow the tournaments you care about -- FIFA World Cup, UEFA Champions League, Euro, Copa America, Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, and more. Multiple events can run simultaneously, each with its own leaderboard and prize pool.

BUILT FOR SPORTS FANS

XEX Play is free to play. No entry fees, no pay-to-win mechanics. Your sports knowledge and strategic skip management are the only advantages. The game is designed around genuine decision-making, not luck.

CONNECTED TO XEX EXCHANGE

Sign in with your XEX Exchange account for a seamless experience. No separate registration required. Token rewards earned in XEX Play flow directly to your exchange account, bridging entertainment and real value.

KEY FEATURES
- 15 daily prediction cards with swipe-based gameplay
- Three card tiers: Gold, Silver, and White with distinct risk/reward profiles
- 40-second decision timer on every card
- Daily, weekly, tournament, and all-time leaderboards
- Mini-leagues for private competitions with friends
- Streak system with milestone rewards
- Real token rewards redeemable on XEX Exchange
- Achievements and shareable badges
- Support for multiple concurrent sports tournaments
- Multi-language support including English, Persian, Arabic, and more
- Dark mode premium design
- Free to play with no paywalls

Download XEX Play and start predicting today. Your daily 15 cards are waiting.
